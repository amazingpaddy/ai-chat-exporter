The only exporter that perfectly preserves **LaTeX mathematical formulas** in your AI chat exports! Other extensions mess with LaTeX; this one gets it right.

Save and share your technical and scientific conversations from Gemini and ChatGPT as flawless Markdown files or copy them directly to your clipboard.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

🎉 𝗡𝗘𝗪 𝗜𝗡 𝗩𝗘𝗥𝗦𝗜𝗢𝗡 𝟯.𝟮.𝟬 🎉

✅ **Granular Message Selection**: Select exactly which messages to export using checkboxes next to each message.
✅ **Selection Presets**: Instantly select all, none, or only AI responses with a dropdown or quick toggles.
✅ **Export to Clipboard**: Copy your chat as Markdown directly to your clipboard—no file download needed!
✅ **Custom Filename**: Edit the filename before exporting to a file. Filenames are sanitized and auto-filled with a timestamp.
✅ **Cleaner Markdown**: No more "Message N" headings—just your conversation, perfectly formatted.
✅ **Improved UX**: Checkboxes and selection reset after export, and the UI is more intuitive and accessible.
✅ **Bug Fixes**: Selection is preserved after scrolling, and all UI elements work reliably in long chats.

⚙️ 𝗛𝗢𝗪 𝗧𝗢 𝗨𝗦𝗘

➡️ **Exporting a Full or Partial Chat**
✅ Go to Gemini (gemini.google.com) or ChatGPT (chatgpt.com) and open any chat.
✅ Click the **Export Chat** button at the top right of the page.
✅ Use the checkboxes or dropdown to select which messages to export.
✅ Choose to export as a Markdown file (with a custom filename) or copy to clipboard.

➡️ **Hiding the Export Button**
✅ Click the extension icon in your Chrome toolbar.
✅ In the popup, check the box to **Hide export button**.
✅ The button will instantly disappear. Uncheck the box to bring it back!

✅ This extension requires clipboard access. It uses the platform's built-in "copy" function to ensure your chat content, including complex LaTeX formulas, is exported with perfect formatting.
